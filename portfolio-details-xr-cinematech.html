XR Cinema Technology

The XR Cinema technology portfolio project is a remarkable piece of innovation that offers a seamless, artifact-free, and high-quality immersive experience. The project has been extensively tested and can render 360-degree images up to a staggering 16K resolution and 8K for videos. The technology is also equipped with the capability to support 360-degree live-streaming and see-thru video, adding to its immersive experience.

The technology is built on the foundation of seamless and artifact-free mesh generator source codes that have been specially designed to render high-fidelity images. The integration of vertices, uv-texture, normal map, shaders, equirectangular mapping, cross-cube-map, fb-cube-map, and interpolation further improves the quality of the rendered images

Overall, the XR Cinema technology portfolio project is an impressive feat that combines cutting-edge technology with meticulous attention to detail to create a seamless, artifact-free, and immersive cinematic experience. Its ability to render high-quality images and support live-streaming video makes it a game-changer in the world of XR cinema technology.
