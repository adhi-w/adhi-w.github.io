HuroEvolutionTN

I am proud to have been a part of the team that developed HuroEvolutionTN, a biped teen-size humanoid robot designed for competing in various humanoid robot competitions. Our hard work and dedication have paid off, as HuroEvolutionTN has been awarded numerous accolades, including the Best Student Conference Paper at the International Automatic Control Conference (CACS 2015) and 1st place in the Teen-size Technical Challenge at the International RoboCup 2015. Additionally, our robot placed 2nd in the Teen-size Soccer Game category at the same competition.

But our success didn't end there - HuroEvolutionTN also achieved great results at the 2015 International Intelligent Humanoid Robotics Competition, where it won 1st place in the Sprint category and placed 2nd in the Marathon, Obstacle Run, and All Round categories in the Adult Size division. These achievements demonstrate our team's technical expertise and ability to work collaboratively to create an advanced humanoid robot capable of performing complex tasks. I am proud to have been a part of this project and look forward to continued success in future competitions.
